Chinese Tomato Egg Stir-Fry (番茄炒蛋)

This is such an easy recipe for an everyday meal, and ensures that you can have a healthy meal even during busy times

Ingredients

  • 1 scallion, whites and greens separated
  • 2 small to medium tomatoes, diced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • salt and white pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs. In a bowl, beat the eggs with the sesame oil, a pinch of salt and white pepper (if using). Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add 1/2 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are just set but still soft and slightly runny. Transfer the eggs to a plate and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1/2 tablespoon of oil. Add the whites of the scallion and sauté for about 20-30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomato and cook for about 2 minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Add the ketchup, stirring well to combine. If the sauce is too thick, add a tablespoon of water to adjust the consistency.
  5. Simmer for another 1-2 minutes until the tomatoes release their juices and the sauce becomes rich and flavorful.
  6. Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet with the tomato mixture.
  7. Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
